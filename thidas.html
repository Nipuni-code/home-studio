    Hello, I'm Thidas De Silva

    I am Thidas, the official Beat Maker of Home Studio Records, a renowned recording studio and production house based in Gampola. With my technical expertise and creative flair, I play a pivotal role in shaping the sonic identity of our label. My passion lies in crafting beats and compositions that resonate with audiences both locally and internationally, contributing to the artistic vision and success of Home Studio Records.

    A music producer driven by a deep-seated passion for innovation, creativity, and collaboration. I believe in the transformative power of music to transcend boundaries and unite people from diverse backgrounds. My goal is to create beats and compositions that resonate with listeners on a visceral level, inspiring and uplifting them.
